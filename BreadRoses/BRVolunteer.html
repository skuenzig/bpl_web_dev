BREAD & ROSES Soup Kitchen

Bread & Roses's purpose is to ease the hunger of those in need. We serve a full-course, nutritious dinner five nights per week. Everyone is welcome. There are no questions asked, no forms to fill out and no charge for the meal. An average of 200 meals are served to homeless or needy families and individuals each evening.

You can volunteer:

Cook

Buy, prep and cook meals.

Server

Serve meals to our guests who will be seated at a dining table.

Greeter

Welcome our guests at the door.
