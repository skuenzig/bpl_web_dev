BREAD & ROSES Soup Kitchen

Bread & Roses is a soup kitchen in Lawrence, MA.
Completely supported by private donations
All in need are welcome to our center to partake of a community meal in a peaceful, friendly environment. Since hunger relief is our main concern, there is no cost for the meal.
